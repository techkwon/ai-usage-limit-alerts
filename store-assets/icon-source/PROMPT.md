## Prompt

Create a highly memorable square browser-extension app icon for an AI usage and quota alert tracker. Use one bold circular quota gauge with four chunky colored segments, a bright white central needle, and a tiny alert pulse. Keep a flat vector-like geometric style, strong silhouette, crisp edges, generous padding, and clarity at 16 px. Use a deep midnight-navy base with white, emerald, terracotta, cyan-blue, and muted-violet accents. Do not include text, letters, numbers, provider logos, trademarks, mascots, faces, watermarks, thin lines, or a generic U-shaped mark.
